Conclusion and Insights from this Analysis

Confirmed Cases: There was a significant decrease in the maximum confirmed cases from 823,225 in 2020 to 414,188 in 2021...

Deaths: There was a notable increase in the maximum death cases from 3,752 in 2020 to 7,374 in 2021...

Recovered Cases: There was a significant decrease in the maximum recovered cases from 1,123,456 in 2020 to 422,436 in 2021...

Trend in Total Deaths: Total deaths increased significantly from January 2020, peaking in January 2021 with 401,893 deaths.After January 2021, total deaths decreased, reaching 132,657 by June 2021.

Average Deaths -: Average deaths per day increased consistently throughout 2020, peaking in January 2021.Post-January 2021, average deaths remained high but showed a decreasing trend by June 2021.

Peak Months January 2021: Highest total deaths (401,893) marking the peak of the pandemic followed by April and May 2021 total deaths (362,387 and 366,549) with significant daily fluctuations.
